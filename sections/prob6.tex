\begin{homeworkProblem}

Given inner product space $\mathbf{V}$, linear operator $T$ on $\mathbf{V}$ and
vector $x$ in $\mathbf{V}$
\[
\begin{aligned}
    &\mathbf{V} = \mathbb{C}^2\\
    &T(z_1, z_2) = (2z_1 + iz_2, (1-i)z_1)\\
    &x = (3-i, 1+2i)
\end{aligned}
\]

Evaluate $T^*$ at the vector $x$.

\solution

Take the standard ordered basis for $\mathbb{C}^2$ and write \[
[T]_\beta = \begin{pmatrix}
    2 & i \\
    1-i & 0
\end{pmatrix}
\Longrightarrow
[T^*]_\beta = [T]_\beta^* = \begin{pmatrix}
    2 & 1+i \\
    -i & 0
\end{pmatrix}
\]

Then \[
\begin{aligned}
T^*(x) &= [T]_\beta^* x \\
&= \begin{pmatrix}
    2 & 1+i \\
    -i & 0
\end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix}
    3-i \\
    1+ 2i
\end{pmatrix}\\
&= \begin{pmatrix}
    5 +i \\
    -1 -3i
\end{pmatrix}
\end{aligned}
\]

\end{homeworkProblem}
